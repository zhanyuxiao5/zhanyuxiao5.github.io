I am Yuxiao Zhan, an artist working across painting, computational arts, and design. With a background in Fine Art and Computational Arts, my practice moves between traditional and digital media.

I use painting, interactive installations, and generative processes to explore how time, perception, and cultural experience take shape. Many of my works begin with small, everyday observations and expand into open-ended, interdisciplinary experiments.

For me, art is not about providing fixed answers, but about opening space for people to look, feel, and think in their own way.
